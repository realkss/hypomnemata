title: On the Keeper
---

> This notebook is kept in the older sense of the word: not as confession, but as ordered attention.

The keeper of **Hypomnemata** is not presented here as a personality to be followed, but as the responsible intelligence behind the arrangement, maintenance, and extension of the archive.

## Office

The keeper's role is threefold:

- to collect material worth sustained return
- to order it so that relations become visible
- to revise it as understanding deepens

What appears here should therefore be read less as finished declaration than as disciplined working record.

## Scope

The notebook gathers material chiefly from:

- theoretical and mathematical physics
- mathematics in support of physical reasoning
- 3D graphics and computation
- adjacent questions in artificial intelligence

## Tone

The site avoids the idiom of personal broadcast. Its preference is for notes, structures, references, and conceptual continuity. Where the individual appears, it is through judgment, selection, and care rather than self-display.

## Use

Readers may treat the notebook as a catalogue, a map, or a sequence of linked fragments. It is intended to reward return rather than immediate completion.
