The lexicon gathers short orienting entries: terms, schools, authors, and recurring concepts that appear throughout the notebook. It is meant to function less as a dictionary than as a small apparatus of clarification.

## Entries To Be Expanded

- **Hypomnemata**: notebooks of recollection, excerpting, and ordered return.
- **Commonplace book**: a disciplined archive of extracts and reflections arranged for reuse.
- **Natural philosophy**: the older name for the study of nature before the later division into sciences.
- **Commentary**: a form of study that advances by careful return to prior texts.
- **Scholium**: a marginal or supplementary remark appended to a larger argument.

## Function

As the notebook grows, this page will branch into shorter entries so that technical notes can rely on a stable vocabulary instead of repeatedly redefining first terms.
