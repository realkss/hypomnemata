This branch gathers the conceptual side of 3D graphics: notes that clarify principles, definitions, derivations, and the mathematical or physical ideas that support later implementation.

## What belongs here

- rendering theory and light transport
- geometry, transforms, and spatial reasoning
- simulation and physically based models
- explanatory notes that support projects elsewhere in the folder

## Use

Use this section for material that should remain valuable even apart from a particular codebase or experiment.
