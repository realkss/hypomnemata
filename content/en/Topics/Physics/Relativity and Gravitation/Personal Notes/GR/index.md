## Guiding Idea

The sequence is meant to preserve continuity. Exact solutions should not detach from geometric principles, and frontier questions should not float free of the classical framework that gives them content. The Hawking-radiation note now serves as one explicit bridge between these domains: classical collapse geometry on the one hand, quantum particle creation on the other.
