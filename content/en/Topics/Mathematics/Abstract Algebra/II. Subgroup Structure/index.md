With the group axioms in hand, this part asks: what smaller groups live inside a given group, and how can we describe the simplest ones? The subgroup criterion, cyclic groups, and generating sets answer those questions.

## Sequence

1. [Subgroups](./05.%20Subgroups) — the $xy^{-1}$ criterion and intersections of subgroups.
2. [Cyclic Groups](./06.%20Cyclic%20Groups) — groups generated by a single element; order and generators.
3. [Generators and Cayley Digraphs](./07.%20Generators%20and%20Cayley%20Digraphs) — finitely generated groups and graph visualisation.
