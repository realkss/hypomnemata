A **generator** is an element, or more generally a set of elements, from which the whole group can be built.

If `G = <g>`, then `g` is a generator of the cyclic group `G`.

More generally, a subset `S` generates `G` if every element of `G` can be written as a finite word in elements of `S` and their inverses.

## One-line intuition

Generators are the building blocks from which the whole group is produced.
