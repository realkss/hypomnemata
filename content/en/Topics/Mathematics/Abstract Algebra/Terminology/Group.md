A **group** is a set `G` together with a binary operation satisfying:

- closure
- associativity
- existence of an identity
- existence of inverses for every element

If the operation also satisfies `ab = ba` for all `a,b`, the group is **abelian**.

## One-line intuition

A group is the smallest algebraic setting in which you can compose, undo, and solve equations reliably.
