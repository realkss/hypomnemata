This sprint uses the concrete range **April 6, 2026 through April 14, 2026**.

If your midterm is on **April 14, 2026**, that gives you **eight preparation days plus exam day**.

## Daily rhythm

- `Desk block`: read the chapter page and do at least one proof cleanly.
- `Commute block`: drill only the matching chapter cards in the [flashcard deck](./midterm-flashcards.html).
- `Close block`: write one theorem statement and one counterexample from memory.

## Monday, April 6, 2026

- Chapters [1](./Chapter%2001%20-%20Introduction%20and%20Examples.md) and [2](./Chapter%2002%20-%20Binary%20Operations.md)
- Goal: separate closure, associativity, identity, and inverse perfectly.

## Tuesday, April 7, 2026

- Chapters [3](./Chapter%2003%20-%20Isomorphic%20Binary%20Structures.md) and [4](./Chapter%2004%20-%20Groups.md)
- Goal: understand isomorphism and basic group manipulations.

## Wednesday, April 8, 2026

- Chapters [5](./Chapter%2005%20-%20Subgroups.md) and [6](./Chapter%2006%20-%20Cyclic%20Groups.md)
- Goal: use subgroup tests quickly and classify cyclic structure.

## Thursday, April 9, 2026

- Chapters [7](./Chapter%2007%20-%20Generators%20and%20Cayley%20Digraphs.md) and [8](./Chapter%2008%20-%20Groups%20of%20Permutations.md)
- Goal: think in generators and read cycle notation fluently.

## Friday, April 10, 2026

- Chapters [9](./Chapter%2009%20-%20Orbits,%20Cycles,%20and%20the%20Alternating%20Groups.md) and [10](./Chapter%2010%20-%20Cosets%20and%20the%20Theorem%20of%20Lagrange.md)
- Goal: move cleanly between parity, cosets, and divisibility arguments.

## Saturday, April 11, 2026

- Chapters [11](./Chapter%2011%20-%20Direct%20Products%20and%20Finitely%20Generated%20Abelian%20Groups.md) and [12](./Chapter%2012%20-%20Plane%20Isometries.md)
- Goal: classify standard abelian groups and identify plane isometries fast.

## Sunday, April 12, 2026

- Chapters [13](./Chapter%2013%20-%20Homomorphisms.md) and [14](./Chapter%2014%20-%20Factor%20Groups.md)
- Goal: connect kernels, images, normality, and quotient structure.

## Monday, April 13, 2026

- Chapter [15](./Chapter%2015%20-%20Factor-Group%20Computations%20and%20Simple%20Groups.md)
- Goal: quotient computations, simple groups, and full cumulative review.

## Tuesday, April 14, 2026

- Light review only.
- Drill definitions, theorem hypotheses, and counterexamples.
- No new material.
