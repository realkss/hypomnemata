This sprint uses the concrete range **April 6, 2026 through April 14, 2026**.

If your midterm is on **April 14, 2026**, that gives you **eight preparation days plus exam day**.

## Daily rhythm

- `Desk block`: read the chapter page and do at least one proof cleanly.
- `Commute block`: drill only the matching chapter cards in the [flashcard deck](/static/algebra/midterm-flashcards.html).
- `Close block`: write one theorem statement and one counterexample from memory.

## Monday, April 6, 2026

- Chapters [1](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/I.-Foundations/01.-Introduction-and-Examples) and [2](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/I.-Foundations/02.-Binary-Operations)
- Goal: separate closure, associativity, identity, and inverse perfectly.

## Tuesday, April 7, 2026

- Chapters [3](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/I.-Foundations/03.-Isomorphic-Binary-Structures) and [4](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/I.-Foundations/04.-Groups)
- Goal: understand isomorphism and basic group manipulations.

## Wednesday, April 8, 2026

- Chapters [5](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/II.-Subgroup-Structure/05.-Subgroups) and [6](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/II.-Subgroup-Structure/06.-Cyclic-Groups)
- Goal: use subgroup tests quickly and classify cyclic structure.

## Thursday, April 9, 2026

- Chapters [7](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/II.-Subgroup-Structure/07.-Generators-and-Cayley-Digraphs) and [8](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/III.-Permutations/08.-Groups-of-Permutations)
- Goal: think in generators and read cycle notation fluently.

## Friday, April 10, 2026

- Chapters [9](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/III.-Permutations/09.-Orbits,-Cycles,-and-the-Alternating-Groups) and [10](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/IV.-Counting-and-Structure/10.-Cosets-and-the-Theorem-of-Lagrange)
- Goal: move cleanly between parity, cosets, and divisibility arguments.

## Saturday, April 11, 2026

- Chapters [11](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/IV.-Counting-and-Structure/11.-Direct-Products-and-Finitely-Generated-Abelian-Groups) and [12](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/IV.-Counting-and-Structure/12.-Plane-Isometries)
- Goal: classify standard abelian groups and identify plane isometries fast.

## Sunday, April 12, 2026

- Chapters [13](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/V.-Homomorphisms-and-Quotients/13.-Homomorphisms) and [14](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/V.-Homomorphisms-and-Quotients/14.-Factor-Groups)
- Goal: connect kernels, images, normality, and quotient structure.

## Monday, April 13, 2026

- Chapter [15](/en/Topics/Mathematics/Abstract-Algebra/V.-Homomorphisms-and-Quotients/15.-Factor-Group-Computations-and-Simple-Groups)
- Goal: quotient computations, simple groups, and full cumulative review.

## Tuesday, April 14, 2026

- Light review only.
- Drill definitions, theorem hypotheses, and counterexamples.
- No new material.
