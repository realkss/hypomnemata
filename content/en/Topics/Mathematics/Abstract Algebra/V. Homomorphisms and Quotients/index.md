The final part introduces maps between groups. Homomorphisms, their kernels and images, the normality criterion, and factor-group construction form a single arc that ends with simple groups — the atoms of group theory.

## Sequence

3. [Factor-Group Computations and Simple Groups](./15.%20Factor-Group%20Computations%20and%20Simple%20Groups) — practical computations and groups with no proper nontrivial normal subgroups.
