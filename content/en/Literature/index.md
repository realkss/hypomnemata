> "Life can only be understood backwards; but it must be lived forwards."  
> -- Soren Kierkegaard

Literature enters the notebook not as ornament but as another discipline of attention. It belongs wherever language, form, memory, and interpretation shape how a world is understood.

## Emphases

- close reading
- style, rhetoric, and form
- literary history and intellectual context
- relations between text, memory, and thought

## Use

This section can hold reading notes, brief commentaries, excerpts worth returning to, and reflections on works that matter to the larger life of the notebook.
